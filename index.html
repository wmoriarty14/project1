Like the feeling Ariel had when she just stepped on land for the first time. Since coming out I’ve kind of made my own family with the people that I surround myself with. Not saying my parents or sibling aren’t supportive we just now live different life styles that its sometimes hard to communicate because they live in their own little bubble and are ignorant to whatever is outside of that bubble… but anyways back my self made Family. The community with the people I surround myself with are so supportive and will do anything to make anyone happy. So the people I know surround myself are my life-line they encourage me, lift my spirit, make me laugh, and I couldn’t thank them enough..
